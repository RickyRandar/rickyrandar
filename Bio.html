Bio

The man behind Ricky Randar

Anda Ngeleza: Who goes by the stage name of Ricky Randar, was born in Port Elizabeth and is currently a 3rd year sound engineering student at Damelin College.

His music career started in 2015. He started out as a House music producer in East London, and in 2016 he moved back to P.E. to start his Sound Engineering course. Early 2017, he produced a song for one of the local female artists, Thembi Mona. The song later went on to win the Best Female Artist award at the South African Upcoming Music Awards in Johannesburg.

During his producing days, he fell in love with Gqom music and decided to tap into the genre as a recording and performing artist. That's when his brand started to get fairly recognized and his career took a turn for the best. It's only then that he was exposed to bigger gigs and started sharing various stages with nationally and internationally established artists like DJ Cndo, Zakes Bantwini, DJ Arch Jnr, Sdudla Noma1000 just to name a few. He worked with a string of talented Gqom music producers and other artists such as DJ Ligwa & Shaya on "Ngik'khuze", DJ Kwaal & TNS, Sdudla Noma1000 on "Ingoma" and "Nomathemba" just to name a few of the producers and recording artists he has worked with in some of the projects.

He's currently working with Life Aimer Productions in terms of management, publishing and digital distribution. His debut EP, Imfihlelo YeSgubhu 2.0, dropped on the 18th of July 2018.
